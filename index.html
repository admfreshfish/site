Our story

Born at a
wedding.

The best photos from my own wedding were sent by friends on WhatsApp — because I was smiling, not posing.

After the wedding, photos ended up scattered across WhatsApp groups, Google Drive and AirDrop. It was chaos. Opah was born to fix that — a single private place for everyone at an event to share what they captured.
